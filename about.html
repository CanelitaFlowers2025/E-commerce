Our Story

The journey of **CANELA ROSE** began in **2020**, inspired by the vision of redefining sportswear for the contemporary woman. We observed a gap in the market: the need for garments that were not only functional for exercise, but also reflected personal style and confidence outside the gym.

Thus, we took our first steps, combining **innovative fabrics** with **figure-enhancing designs**, always prioritizing freedom of movement. From the beginning, our commitment was to create a line that would accompany women in every aspect of their active lives, from an intense workout to a relaxing afternoon.

With each collection, we have evolved, incorporating new technologies and trends, but always maintaining our essence: **empowering women through sports fashion**. We are proud to say that CANELA ROSE is not just a brand; it is a community of strong, elegant, and determined women who support each other.

Looking to the future, we remain committed to **excellence and sustainability**, exploring eco-friendly materials and responsible production processes. Our story is a reflection of our growth alongside you, our valued customers, and we are excited for what's to come.
